About UMMI

一般社団法人 Ummah Muslim Mie Indonesia (UMMI) serves the Indonesian Muslim community in Mie Prefecture, Japan. We are building UMMI Masjid, a permanent home for prayer, learning, and community. Time is moving, and your support can make this lasting legacy a reality.
